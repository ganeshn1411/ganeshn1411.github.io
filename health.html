Health Analytic


The Story Behind

SAP recently launched a new tool SAP Lumira which for efficient visualization and analysis of big data. As part of Business Analysis course project, we integrated this tool and used the data provided by Government of India about the Helath metrics to come up with an intelligent dashboard.

The application, Health analytic, takes data of various states within the country along with factors like type of disease, period, location, environment, food habits and so on. This data is further analysed to find certain pattern. This is further visualized using dashboards for the different metrics.
