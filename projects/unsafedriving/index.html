Unsafe Driving Detection



About

Every year, there are thousands of deaths and hunderd of thousands of accidents that happen due to unsafe driving. Our project tries to combine the concepts of Computer Vision and Machine Learning to build models that can detect unsafe driving.

Results

2 class classification

Our algorithm was able to differentiate 2 classes of safe and unsafe driving very well with an accuracy of 92.4%. The table below shows the truth table values:

Predicted Safe Predicted Unsafe
Actual Safe 106 10
Actual Unsafe 7 101

3 class classification

While the accuracy of our 3 class classification might not look amazing, it still performed more than twice the performance of default classifier. In addition, our goal here is not to increase accuracy but maximize detection of unsafe driving and our algorithm does that well. Below is the truth table for 3 class classification with an accuracy of about 68%:

Predicted Safe Predicted Calling Predicted Make-Up
Actual Safe 42 6 39
Actual Calling 9 81 3
