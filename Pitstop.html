Pitstop


The Story Behind

One of the major problem living in crowed cities like Bangalore in to find parking places, people waste lot of fuel and time looking for places to park.

I made Pitstop android application to solve this problem. The web app accesses your location and shows the parking spots nearby on a google map, it also give you the direction to the parking places , and tells you the chances of finding a free spot at different places using crowd sourced data.
